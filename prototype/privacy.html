Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

DataPeaks Solutions ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website (datapeakssolutions.com) or enquire about our programs, and how we use and protect it.

Information we collect

  • Enquiry details you provide — name, email, phone/WhatsApp, course of interest, and your message.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as pages visited and device/browser type, collected via cookies and analytics tools.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and share relevant course and enrollment information.
  • To improve our website, content, and programs.
  • To send updates about DataPeaks programs where you've asked us to — you can opt out anytime.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers that help us operate (for example, email or analytics), and only as needed to deliver our services, or where required by law.

Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is fully secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and can opt out of marketing messages at any time by contacting us at info@datapeakssolutions.com.

Public project content

Project code we publish uses only open-source or publicly available datasets. We never publish proprietary student data or paid-course materials.
